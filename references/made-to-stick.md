# Made to Stick (SUCCES) Framework
**Core Concept:** Ideas must survive the "Curse of Knowledge."

### The SUCCES Principles
- **Simple:** Find the core. If you say three things, you say nothing. Use analogies.
- **Unexpected:** Break a pattern. Create a "knowledge gap" the audience wants to fill.
- **Concrete:** Use sensory language. No "synergy"—use "handles 1k invoices."
- **Credible:** Use "Sinatra Test" (If you can make it there...) or vivid details.
- **Emotional:** Make them care. Appeal to their identity (Who do they want to be?).
- **Stories:** Mental simulation. Stories act as "flight simulators" for the brain.

### Verification Checklist
- Would a 10-year-old understand the core benefit?
- Is there a "Surprise" element to keep them engaged?
