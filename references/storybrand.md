# StoryBrand (SB7) Framework
**Core Concept:** The customer is the Hero; the Brand is the Guide.

### The 7 Elements
1. **The Hero:** Define exactly what the customer wants.
2. **The Problem:** - External: The physical problem (e.g., "Manual data entry").
   - Internal: How it makes them feel (e.g., "Overwhelmed/Anxious").
   - Philosophical: Why it's "wrong" (e.g., "You shouldn't waste talent on robots' work").
3. **The Guide:** Express Empathy ("We've been there") and Authority (Stats/Results).
4. **The Plan:** Give a clear 3-step path (e.g., 1. Audit, 2. Implement, 3. Scale).
5. **Call to Action:** Direct ("Buy Now") vs Transitional ("Download Guide").
6. **Avoid Failure:** What is at stake if they don't act?
7. **Success:** Paint a vivid picture of their life after the solution.

### Verification Checklist
- Is the brand talking about itself too much? (If yes, rewrite as the Guide).
- Is the "Plan" simple enough to understand in 5 seconds?
